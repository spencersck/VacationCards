London, England

London is the capital and larget city of England and the United Kingdom. It stands on the River Thames in south-east England at the head of a 50-mile estuary to the North Sea.

Maui, Hawaii

The island of Maui is the second largest of the islands of the state of Hawaii at 727.2 square miles and is the 17th largest island in the United States.

Las Vegas, Nevada

Las Vegas is an internationally renowned major resort city, known for its gambling, shopping, fine dining, entertainment, and nightlife. The Las Vegas Valley as a whole serves as the leading financial, commerical, and cultural center for Nevada.

New Orleans, Louisiana

New Orleans is world renowned for its distinctive music, creole cuisine, unique dialects, and its annual celebration festivals. The historic heart of the city is the French Quarter, known for its French and Spanish Creole architecture and vibrant nightlife along Bourbon Street.

Rome, Italy

Rome is the capital of Italy as well as being the capital of the Lazio region, the centre of the Metropolitan City of Rome. Rome is the country's most populated comune and the third most populous city in the European Union by population within city limits. Is considered to be the cradle of Western Christian culture and civilization, and the center of the Catholic Church.
